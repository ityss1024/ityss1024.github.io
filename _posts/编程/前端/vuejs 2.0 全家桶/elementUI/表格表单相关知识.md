

**1.表格添加点击事件**

参考 https://blog.csdn.net/weixin_43900414/article/details/108753774

添加 一列 

```javascript
<el-table-column 
        prop="nocount" 
        label="验证未通过数量" 
        align='center' 
        width="10%">
          <template slot-scope="scope">
              <div @click="detile(scope.row)">{{ scope.row.nocount }}</div>
          </template>
  </el-table-column>
```
